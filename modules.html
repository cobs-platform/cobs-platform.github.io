decide_come()[source]¶

Each person need to decide if he/she will come to work today, when exactly they come, and when exactly they leave. Each person target to come at 8:30 am and leave at 5 pm, with a exponential distribution where mu = 30 minute. Use exponential distribution because people tend to come/leave on time with a very small chance of being late. In the meantime, assume that people directly goes into their own office right away. They require some time to walk to their office.

Returns:True if come to work, False otherwise
